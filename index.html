As an extra something, we wrote you a little guide on how to get ready for tomorrow, so that everything goes A-Okay:

 

Find the coziest spot and get it ready.

A cushy couch or the hard back of your chair—you’re the boss. And if you have an awesome balcony or a snug coffee shop nearby, live your best life! And make sure the connection is solid to avoid any curveballs tomorrow.

 

Share the event with people you like.

Ask your friends and colleagues to join. Side note: each of you might want to use their own device to join—that way you’ll be able to chat and play together. And as for the stream, you can easily watch it together. But what if you don’t want to get sidetracked by your friends? Easy. Watch the stream with your pet.

 

Make sure you have everything you need.

You will definitely need good headphones, a microphone to use voice chat, and a camera to network. What else? Depends on what you like: a thermos of coffee or a cooler of beer; drawing the curtains to hide from the sun or getting cozy under a warm blanky. Ask yourself, “What could help me spend a few days with Geecko and enjoy them the most?”

 

Switch off the notifications that might distract you.

Spending quality time is everything! Pick the activities you like in advance and don’t let anything else distract you. P.S. This, of course, doesn’t apply to workplace notifications. That we’ll have to live with :)
